Munshiganj Polytechnic Institute

Munshiganj Polytechnic Institute is a government polytechnic institute located in Mirkadim, Munshiganj, Bangladesh. It was established in 2006. This institute conducts 4 years Diploma-in-Engineering course under Bangladesh Technical Education Board.

Visit us to know more

Courses we offer

Munshiganj Polytechnic Institute offers a range of diploma courses in engineering and technology. With a focus on practical skills and industry relevance, the institute's programs cover disciplines such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering. The curriculum includes theoretical learning, hands-on laboratory sessions, and industrial training to prepare students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Computer Engineering

Munshiganj Polytechnic Institute's Computer Department provides a dynamic learning environment for students interested in technology. The department offers a diploma course in Computer Engineering, emphasizing hardware, software, and networking. Students gain practical skills in areas such as computer systems, software development, and network management.

Civil Engineering

Munshiganj Polytechnic Institute's Civil Department offers a focused diploma course in Civil Engineering. Students in this program learn essential principles of construction, structural design, and infrastructure development. The department provides a well-rounded education, blending theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, preparing students for successful careers in the field of civil engineering.

Electrical Engineering

Munshiganj Polytechnic Institute's Electrical Department offers a dynamic diploma course in Electrical Engineering. Focused on practical skills in electronics, power systems, and renewable energy, students gain hands-on experience in electrical circuit design. Faculty expertise and industry insights ensure graduates are well-prepared for diverse roles in the field.
